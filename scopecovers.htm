Endocover Sheath for Flexible endoscopes

New British-made Endocover sheaths minimise patient risk and reduce operational costs. These sterile, latex-free sheaths protect endoscopes from damage by chemicals, as well as minimising staff exposure to hazards. They have been successfully tested for impermeability to V-CJD prions.

For a copy of the test report, click here

The innovative sheath material and protective tube facilitate smooth application and removal, and the new lens profile gives maximum optical integrity. Finally, unique colour-coding of each sheath ensures that the correct style is selected, eliminating unnecessary wastage.
