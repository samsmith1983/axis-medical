SurgiFish® Viscera Retainer

Closure of abdominal wounds sometimes presents difficulties, especially when the patient is obese, or when relaxation of the abdominal wall is less than ideal.

In such situations the use of a viscera retainer can be helpful. SurgiFish® offers considerable benefits:

  • It is manufactured from bio-compatible silicone elastomer.
  • It is supplied pre-sterilised.
  • Its shape is suitable for all sizes of incision, and it can be cut if necessary.
