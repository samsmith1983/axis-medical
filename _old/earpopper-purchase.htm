Ear Popper

The Ear Popper is designed to treat some common ear problems as an alternative to medication or surgery, in both children and adults. It can be appropriate for treating conditions caused by middle ear pressure such as:

  • Middle ear fluid (Otitis Media with effusion)
  • Eustachian Tube Dysfunction.
  • Hearing Loss.
  • Ear Pain and pressure caused by air travel or diving.
  • Ear fullness caused by colds, allergies and sinusitis.
