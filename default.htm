Axis Medical is 20!

Axis Medical was founded in 1996 by its Managing Director, Peter J. Smith, previously European Director of Xomed Inc.

Axis Medical specialises in consumable products for Otolaryngology and Plastic Surgery, and has Independent Sales Representatives in England, Scotland and Wales.
