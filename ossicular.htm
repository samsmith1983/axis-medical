Ossicular Prostheses

The patented Grace Medical ALTO (Adjustable Length Titanium Ossicular Prosthesis) line is the only non-crimp adjustable length titanium design available. This unique design allows the prosthesis to be adjusted even after its initial sizing, trimming and placement: it can be lengthened or shortened in precise 0.25mm increments.
